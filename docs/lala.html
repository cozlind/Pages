LALA

LALA is a 3D puzzle game that created for Shanghai GameJam 2015.

The theme is the hashtag sign #, which we interpreted as the separation of the space by lines and faces. It's an idea of turing the two dimensional space into a three dimension one. The mechanism for the game is simply constructing a route by pulling up the cubes and have the little white sphere to reach to her destination.
