MDN Intelligent Decoration

MDN Intelligent Decoration is a platform for customer to draw the house layout they like.

Features :

  1. Draw walles and drag furniture freely
  2. Download the furniture models from online database which is matching with the commodities existed
  3. Put the door on the wall freely with the door open
  4. Draw floor and celling pinned to the wall point
  5. Connect wall point automaticlly
  6. Draw Bezier-curve-wall
  7. Choose the wallpaper
  8. Choose the texture for floor and ceilling
  9. Put the light on the ceilling
  10. Save layouts for every customer
  11. Users can walk around inside after complete their layout
  12. Users can walk around with VR device

Platform : PC/Android/Oculus Rift Dk2

During my internship in ZK3D, Inc, I developed MDN Intelligent Decoration with my colleagues in two month. As the lead programmer, I developed most part of this software, specifically, everything except server connection and asset download. And I also bore some UI works.
